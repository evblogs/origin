ElectricPe: India’s Smart EV Charging Network for Homes, Cities & Fleets (2025 Guide)

June 14, 2025

Introduction: India’s EV Push Needs Smart Charging

India’s electric vehicle revolution is in full swing. From two-wheelers buzzing through traffic to electric buses gliding on city routes, the shift is unmistakable. But one major roadblock remains: reliable and accessible EV charging. That’s where ElectricPe steps in, aiming to change how India charges its electric vehicles—making it faster, smarter, and more affordable.

If you’re an EV owner or just considering the switch, this blog will give you a complete understanding of how ElectricPe is shaping the future of e-mobility in India.

Who Is ElectricPe? – The Brand Behind the Buzz

Founded in 2021, ElectricPe is a Bengaluru-based EV charging platform on a mission to make clean mobility accessible to every Indian. Their approach is straightforward but ambitious: build the country’s largest and most reliable EV charging network by collaborating with existing infrastructure owners, like kirana stores, malls, apartments, fuel stations, and commercial complexes.

The company doesn’t believe in owning and building massive infrastructure from scratch. Instead, they smartly aggregate and digitize already existing power sources and locations—making them accessible to the EV public through one easy-to-use platform.

Founders’ Vision and Company Background

ElectricPe was co-founded by Avinash Sharma (former Ola and Bounce exec) and Rakesh Sharma, with deep expertise in mobility, logistics, and energy. Their vision was clear: most Indians won’t switch to electric unless charging becomes as easy as filling petrol. They aimed to bridge the “last-mile” charging gap for urban EV users.

Under their leadership, ElectricPe has grown from a pilot project in Bangalore to a multi-city smart charging ecosystem trusted by thousands of EV riders.

Why ElectricPe Stands Out in India’s EV Landscape

ElectricPe is not just another charging company. It’s building a software-first solution that makes EV charging:

  • Frictionless – Book, unlock, and pay for charging—all through one app.
  • Affordable – Dynamic pricing models ensure low cost for consumers.
  • Distributed – Chargers are available not just at malls, but also in your street’s tea shop or apartment basement.
  • Data-Driven – Usage insights help optimize availability, pricing, and grid load.

This asset-light model means faster scaling and wider reach—especially in urban India where space is a constraint.

The ElectricPe App – How It Works and What It Offers

At the heart of ElectricPe’s solution is its mobile app, available on Android and iOS. Here’s what the app offers:

  • Live Map of Nearby Electric Vehicle Charging Stations
    Whether you’re in Bengaluru, Delhi, or Mumbai, just open the app and see all available Electric Vehicle Charging Stations with real-time availability.
  • Station Booking & Access
    You can pre-book charging slots or simply walk-in and scan the QR code at the station.
  • Cashless Payment Options
    UPI, credit/debit cards, wallets—everything is integrated.
  • Charging Analytics
    Track your vehicle’s charging sessions, cost per kWh, and energy usage patterns.
  • In-App Support & Feedback
    Raise tickets, rate stations, and view live updates from the ElectricPe team.

Where Is ElectricPe Available? – Current Network Map

As of mid-2025, ElectricPe has over 15,000 charging points across India. Their strongest presence is in:

  • Bangalore
  • Delhi NCR
  • Mumbai
  • Hyderabad
  • Pune
  • Chennai

They are rapidly expanding into Tier-2 cities like Jaipur, Indore, Lucknow, and Coimbatore through strategic local partnerships.

Partnerships and Collaborations – A Big Force Behind Their Growth

ElectricPe’s biggest strength lies in its partnerships. These include:

  • Smart parking operators – Integrating chargers within parking lots.
  • Housing societies – Private EV parking solutions.
  • Retail chains – electric vehicle Charging stations at stores like Reliance Fresh and Spencer’s.
  • Battery swapping firms – Interlinking infrastructure for e-rickshaws.

In 2024, ElectricPe also announced a partnership with Indian Railways, installing electric vehicle charging stations at select railway parking zones.

Smart Charging & Load Management – What Makes ElectricPe Tech-Savvy

ElectricPe uses AI-based load balancing to avoid grid overload during peak hours. Their backend systems analyze:

  • Local energy demand
  • Charger availability
  • Real-time user density

This enables smart energy distribution, reduced power loss, and better uptime, even in high-demand areas like IT parks or residential societies.

Sustainability Goals – Green Charging & Grid Optimization

ElectricPe is committed to India’s net-zero goals. They integrate:

  • Solar-powered stations in Tier-1 locations
  • Green energy sourcing partnerships with renewable providers
  • Smart energy dashboards for housing societies to monitor carbon impact

Their goal is to ensure 60% of all charging on their network is powered by renewables by 2027.

Pricing, Plans, and User Benefits

Unlike others, ElectricPe doesn’t have a one-size-fits-all model. They offer:

  • Pay-per-use (₹3.5–₹5 per kWh)
  • Monthly charging plans (₹499–₹1499)
  • Bulk subscription for fleet operators

Users benefit from:

  • Loyalty rewards
  • First 5 charges free on sign-up
  • Referral bonuses

Fleet operators get dashboard access to monitor usage, billing, and savings.

User Experience – Real Feedback from Daily EV Users

Here’s what some daily users say:

  • “I use an Ola S1 and stay in HSR Layout. ElectricPe’s kirana station near my home is a lifesaver.” – Ramesh, Bengaluru
  • “Unlike other apps, this one never hangs. Booking is super fast.” – Priya, Delhi
  • “As a fleet manager for 20 EV autos, ElectricPe’s dashboard helps me save ₹15,000/month in operations.” – Sameer, Pune

The focus on reliability and usability is a huge win in a market that’s often riddled with faulty or busy chargers.

Challenges and How ElectricPe Is Solving Them

Some challenges ElectricPe tackles include:

  • Charger misuse – Solved with smart locks and user identity verification.
  • Grid overload – Tackled with AI-based energy balancing.
  • EV parking congestion – Coordinated parking partners to reserve charging slots.
  • Lack of awareness – Partnering with local RTOs and dealerships to spread awareness about EV charging.

ElectricPe’s Role in Public and Commercial Charging

ElectricPe isn’t just for private vehicle owners. Their B2B offering includes:

  • Bulk installations for delivery fleets (Zomato, Swiggy, Dunzo)
  • Integration into logistics hubs
  • EV parking plus charging models in corporate parks

This dual focus on B2C and B2B makes their model highly scalable.

Recent Updates and Growth Milestones (2024–2025)

  • Reached 1 million charges milestone in Q4 2024
  • Expanded into 6 new cities including Nagpur, Bhopal, and Kochi
  • Raised $18M in Series B funding to scale infrastructure
  • Launched ‘ElectricPe SolarPods’, portable solar EV charging for events and campuses

Future Roadmap – What’s Coming by 2030?

ElectricPe has publicly announced some bold targets:

  • 1 lakh charging points by 2030
  • Integration with EV toll roads and highways
  • Launch of battery swapping lockers for last-mile delivery fleets
  • EV Travel Planner for intercity routes in the app
  • Smart vehicle integration for auto-charging session start

Why ElectricPe Could Be the Game-Changer for India’s EV Ecosystem

India’s EV market is fragmented and still maturing. What sets ElectricPe apart is its inclusive, low-cost, and tech-driven approach.

They don’t just serve Tesla-like customers. They cater to college kids on e-bikes, delivery boys on scooters, and office-goers with Tata Nexon EVs—all through a network built around daily Indian life.

How to Join, Locate, and Start Using ElectricPe Today

Getting started is easy:

  1. Download the ElectricPe app (Android/iOS)
  2. Sign up and add your EV type
  3. View nearby stations
  4. Book a slot or walk-in
  5. Scan and charge!

You’ll also get updates on new station launches and special discounts based on your location.

Conclusion: Building India’s Future with Every Charge

ElectricPe isn’t just building chargers. It’s building confidence among EV users, breaking myths, and making clean mobility an everyday reality.

If India’s future is electric, companies like ElectricPe are laying the power lines—quietly, reliably, and smartly.
